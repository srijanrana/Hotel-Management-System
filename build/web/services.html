Services

Small Hotel Royal maintains the best of traditional values while continually introducing new service standards and the latest technology as befits the modern world. Guests have the assurance of friendly and highly professional staff combined with the advantage of all the latest business and entertainment systems, in rooms and throughout the hotel.

Thanks to its refurbishment, completed in 2000, Small Hotel Royal offers the necessary facilities and equipment to welcome disabled clients in the best conditions. An adapted entrance, lifts and six rooms were especially designed for them.

Complimentary services include:
  • Gourmet extended continental breakfast
  • Morning newspaper delivered to your room
  • Coffee and tea available 24 hours a day in the Sitting Room, with handmade biscotti served at three o'clock in the afternoon
  • Weekday morning chauffeur service to select destinations
  • We offer six bicycles for your use, complete with helmets, locks, backpacks and water bottles
  • Wireless internet access
  • Plentiful street parking available
Services available for an additional charge:
  • Complete Room Service menu available 24 hours a day
  • Evening and airport chauffeur service
  • Dry cleaning and laundry services
Hotel Amenities
  • Fitness center on site, with strength equipment, and cardio equipment with integrated individual TVs
  • DVD/Blu-ray disc lending library
  • Meeting and event spaces
  • Secretarial services
